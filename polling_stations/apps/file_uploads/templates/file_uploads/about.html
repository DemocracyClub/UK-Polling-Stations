Welcome to Democracy Club’s new polling station uploader tool! We hope this will streamline the process for providing your lists of polling places to us.

If you have any questions please get in touch.

#About the Polling Station Finder

Democracy Club’s WhereDoIVote.co.uk allows voters to find their assigned polling station using nothing more than a postcode. The data you provide also powers the postcode look-up on The Electoral Commission voter pages, and is available for other organisations to use, free of charge.

You can add our polling station widget to your own website for free.

Once you have uploaded your data, our developers check the file for problems and import it into the finder. Once the data is live on the site, we will contact you to let you know using the email address used to upload the file. All data is removed immediately after the election it is assigned to is over. We always err on the side of caution, and will not display information if we are at all unsure about it. Please note the finder does not at present cover town and parish elections, or other forms of local polls (eg. planning referendums).
